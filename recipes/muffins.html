Breakfast Muffins Recipe

Description

This is one of my favorite muffin recipes ever. It is qucik and is great for something early in the morning.

It only takes about 20 minutes and some jam. You will need a pan for baking the muffins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • butter
  • jam

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, suage, baking powder, and salt. Wisk together
  2. Add in eggs, oil, and milk, and mix only until combined. Do not beat or over-mix or else the muffins will come out tough and dry.
  3. Scoop the dough into a muffin pan or into muffin holders.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. The tops should spring back up when you press on them or a toothpick should come out clean.

Feel free to add jam after waiting for the muffins to cool. Enjoy!
