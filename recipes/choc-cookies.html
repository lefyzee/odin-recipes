Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Now what recipe website would be complete without chocolate chip cookies? Here are some delicious cookies that can't wait to be baked! It is only 350 calories per serving and they take about 30 minutes to bake but the dough will need to stay in the freezer.

You will need a baking pan (be sure to grease it), wisk or mixer, and a cooling rack.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 14.0 oz. semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine melted butter and brown sugar in the bowl of a free stadning mixer
  2. Beat until mixture is well mixed
  3. Add vanilla and egg plus yoke. Beat it well
  4. Slowly add flower and salt. Add chocolate.
  5. Chill a minimum of 6 hours and up to 3 days. After one day is the best
  6. Preheat oven to 400°F and place into baking sheet as tiny balls about the size of a tablespoon
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes. They should be golden brown. Leave them in longer for a more chrisp cookie or less for a chewy cookie
  8. Let cool before eating
